PhD Candidate, IT University of Copenhagen, Denmark. My PhD project Hidden Layer Interaction proposes a novel technique for co-creating with generative AI via models' neurons. Bridging the fields of mechanistic interpretability and interaction design, I draw on methods from computer vision and design to move beyond common prompting techniques towards an experiential understanding of AI through interaction.
